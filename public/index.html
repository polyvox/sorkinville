Welcome to Sorkinville!

Community Map

May 22, 2015

The planning commission has updated the community map with the new construction that you may see happening around the town. Never fear! They will make sure that access to our favorite restaurants, parks, and watering holes remain undisturbed during this time of expansion. Vive la Sorkinville!

Mayor tries a new hat and suit

July 17, 2015

Our mayor, Bob Xie, wowed everyone with a stunning new hat and suit combination presented to him by the Sorkinville Rotary Club in commemoration of his twelfth year in office.

Bob said that he plans on wearing it to the next city council meeting where he will lead discussions on the new zoning enhancements proposed for the next phase of the build-out of our fine town.
